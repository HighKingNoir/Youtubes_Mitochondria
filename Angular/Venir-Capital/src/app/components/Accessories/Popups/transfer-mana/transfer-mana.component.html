Approve Bid Contract
To place bids on Sivantis, you need to approve the spending of Polygon Decentraland Mana on Sivantis' Bid Service Smart Contract. Please follow the steps below:
  • Below is the link to allow the tranfer of a set amount of your polygon mana to our smart contract.
  • Once you approve this transaction, you will be able send mana to our bid service smart contract.
  • The allowance is linked to the crypto wallet, not your account with us. If you transact with multiple crypto wallets, you need to set an allowance for each one.
  • Each time you place a bid, you will lower the allowed mana you can send to our bid service smart contract. Heres an example:
Lets say you set an allowance of 500 mana. If you decide to place a bid worth 300 mana, your new allowance will be 200 mana. If you were to place another bid worth 300 mana, you will get notified that you need to increase your allowance. You can view your allowance Here.
